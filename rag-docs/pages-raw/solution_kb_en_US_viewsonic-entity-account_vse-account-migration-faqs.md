It will unify authentication across services such as ClassSwift, TeamOne, and myViewBoard, making it easier for users to manage their digital ecosystem. By aligning all platforms under a single identity provider, we are enhancing data security, streamlining permission handling, and improving long-term scalability across enterprise and educational environments.
  

Do I need to create a new ViewSonic account?
No. Your current myViewBoard account will be automatically migrated. You will use the same login credentials, but access will now route through the new ViewSonic sign-in interface. The transition is seamless, and you wonâ€™t lose your data, subscriptions, or roles in the process.

  * Entity management now handled through the ViewSonic Entity portal
  * Role management moved to product-specific portals

  * cloud.viewsonic.com
  * no-reply@service.viewsonic.cloud


  

Whitelisting these ensures that users receive all important emails (e.g., verification, password resets) and that UI components function properly without being blocked by network restrictions.

  * New entities will only give initial Manager access to the Owner. Additional users must be added inside Manager.


  

This shift enables more tailored access control across ViewSonic services but introduces a need for Admins to adapt to multi-platform role management.
