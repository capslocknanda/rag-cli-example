# Account overview
Your ViewSonic Account settings page allows you to manage how you access ViewSonic products. Additionally, settings you provide here will be used to customize your experience across various ViewSonic products and services.
  

## Access Account settings
You can access your **Account** settings directly by performing the following:
  

  1. Navigate to the following URL:
     * <https://cloud.viewsonic.com/>  
  

  2. Click **Sign in** on the upper right.
  3. On the next page, use your credentials to sign-in.
  4. The next screen should show your **Account** page.


  

  

  

**via myViewBoard.com**
To access **Account** settings while logged in 
  

  1. Sign-in to myviewboard.com.
  2. Click your user avatar on the upper right.
  3. Click **Manage account** on the dropdown.
  4. The next screen should show your **Account** page.


  

  

  

## Account sections
The ViewSonic Account settings page is organized into the following sections. Click on a section to learn more about the available options on each page.
  

|  **[Account](https://www.viewsonic.com/solution/kb/vs-account)**  
Provide details that make it easy for others to identify your account. Note that you must click Save at the bottom of this page to apply your changes.  
---|---  
|  **[Notifications](https://www.viewsonic.com/solution/kb/vs-notifications)**  
Specify if you want to receive the latest news in myViewBoard via email. These will be sent to the email address you use during sign-in.  
|  **[Security](https://www.viewsonic.com/solution/kb/vs-security)**  
Manage security settings related to signing in using your ViewSonic account. Click on a tab on the right panel to access available options.  
|  **[Subscriptions](https://www.viewsonic.com/solution/kb/vs-subscription)**  
Review and subscribe to plans available for your account, as well as update your personal payment information, transaction history, and recurring payment details.  
|  **Support**  
Opens the ViewSonic support page in a new window. Use the support page to find fixes, troubleshoot issues, and learn more about various ViewSonic products.  
  

  

## Compatible services
Your ViewSonic account grants access to specific features of several online ViewSonic services, which include but are not limited to the following:
  

myViewBoardTeamOneOriginalsCompanionCommunity
  

Note that the list is non-exhaustive and services offered may change without prior notice.
