# AirSync
  

  

  

  

**AirSync** is a wireless screen-sharing solution that enables seamless screen sharing between a user's device and ViewSonic interactive flat panel (IFP) displays. 
  

  * [**AirSync Sender**](https://www.viewsonic.com/solution/kb/en_US/send-your-screen/airsync-sender-client)  
Launch the AirSync Sender app to enable seamless sharing with your IFP. The app also comes with several quality-of-life enhancements that make screen sharing easier.   
  

  * [**AirSync Web Sender**](https://www.viewsonic.com/solution/kb/en_US/send-your-screen/cast-from-airsyncnet)  
No AirSync Sender? Just head over to AirSync Web Sender to share with your IFP. No need to be on the same network.  
  

  * [**Supported IFPs**](https://www.viewsonic.com/solution/kb/en_US/air-sync-troubleshooting/viewboard-support)  
Check if your IFP is compatible with AirSync.  
  

  * [**Download Quick Start Guides**](https://www.viewsonic.com/solution/kb/en_US/send-your-screen/airsync-quick-start-guide)  
Quick guides on how to get AirSync up and running. If you need PDF versions of how to use AirSync, click here.  
  

  * [**FAQs & troubleshooting**](https://www.viewsonic.com/solution/kb/en_US/air-sync-troubleshooting/airsync-faqs)  
We answer top questions frequently asked about AirSync.

  * [**Touchback**](https://www.viewsonic.com/solution/kb/en_US/extra-features/airsync-touchback-)  
Use the IFP's touch controls to remotely control your device ‚Äîas if you were in front of it.  
  

  * [**Split screen**](https://www.viewsonic.com/solution/kb/en_US/extra-features/airsync-split-screen)  
Get 2 or more users to share their screen. Up to 6 users can share their screen at the same time.  
  

  * [**Moderator mode**](https://www.viewsonic.com/solution/kb/en_US/extra-features/airsync-moderator-mode)  
Selectively allow which users can share their screens on the IFP for better control over the sharing session.   
  

  * [**Quick connect**](https://www.viewsonic.com/solution/kb/en_US/extra-features/quick-connect)  
Search for nearby IFPs automatically and connect seamlessly ‚Äîwithout the need to type input codes.  
  

  * [**Screen extension**](https://www.viewsonic.com/solution/kb/en_US/send-your-screen/cast-from-airsync-client)  
Create a virtual screen that the OS treats as an extra monitor where you can move windows and applications you want to share.  
  



üåê Works seamlessly with your network
  * Runs on both Internet and LAN connections. 
  * Compatible with Google Chrome and Microsoft Edge.
  * Broadcast your screen to one or more IFPs in the same network to efficiently present to a large audience. 
  * Supports Windows, macOS, iOS, and Android devices.
  * Supports sharing your screen to the IFP via other protocols:
    * Google Cast
    * Miracast
    * AirPlay
    * Directly from a web browser
