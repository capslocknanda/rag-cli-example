# Manager overview
  

**Manager** is a tool for Entity Admins to remotely manage multiple installations of ViewSonic devices. It supports managing a wide range of devices, which include but not limited to: 
  

  * [ViewBoards](https://manuals.viewsonic.com/ViewSonic_User_Guide_Database)
  * ViewSonic CDEs
  * ViewBoard Boxes


  

**Manager** is only available for users under Entity accounts. Users must be signed in as an **Entity Admin** or **Entity Owner**. If your organization has not yet signed up for an **Entity** account, follow the steps here first: 
  

  * [ Sign up for an Entity account](https://www.viewsonic.com/solution/kb/en_US/before-creating-entity/request-entity-account)


  

## Access Manager
To access **Manager:**
  

  1. Sign in to 
  2. Perform one of the following: 
     * Click the **Manager** tile, or
     * Click the [Entity Management](https://www.viewsonic.com/solution/kb/en_US/entity-management-1/entity-management-overview "Entity Management") tile, and then click **Manager** on the side panel.   
  

  3. The page redirects to Manager's Dashboard screen.


  

  

## What to do next 
Once you have access to **Manager** , we highly recommend reading up on the following topics:
  

Get an initial overview of what you can do with Manager:
  * [myViewBoard Manager features](https://www.viewsonic.com/solution/kb/en_US/managing-devices/manager-features-overview)


  

Use an Advanced license to access special Manager features:
  * [Overview of Manager Advanced](https://www.viewsonic.com/solution/kb/manager-advanced-features/overview-manager-advanced)
  * [Assigning Manager Advanced licenses](https://www.viewsonic.com/solution/kb/manager-advanced-features/assign-licenses)


  

Review which features are supported by your device:
  * [Device capability chart](https://www.viewsonic.com/solution/kb/other-manager-info/device-capability-chart)
  * [Release history](https://www.viewsonic.com/solution/kb/other-manager-info/release-history-m)
