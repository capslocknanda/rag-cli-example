# TeamOne overview
TeamOne is an online collaborative whiteboard designed for:
  * Generating ideas
  * Managing projects
  * Facilitating team collaboration 


  

  

TeamOne users can create boards and utilize various tools to design diagrams, charts, and other layouts. Additionally, TeamOne allows for the creation of projects to efficiently organize boards to support different collaboration styles, namely:
  

  * **Real-time collaboration.**   
Team members can share and interact with boards during meetings.
  * **Asynchronous collaboration.**   
Team members can contribute at their convenience. TeamOne boards are online and accessible all the time.


  

It is fully optimized for use on desktop web browsers and displays with touch support. To get started, refer to the following articles:
  

  * [Access TeamOne](https://www.viewsonic.com/solution/kb/getting-started-with-teamone/how-to-access-teamone)
  * [Create a board](https://www.viewsonic.com/solution/kb/getting-started-with-teamone/how-to-create-a-board-in-teamone)
  * [Share your board](https://www.viewsonic.com/solution/kb/collaborating-with-teamone/how-to-share-a-teamone-board)
  * [Invite others to your team](https://www.viewsonic.com/solution/kb/managing-teams-and-projects-with-teamone/how-to-create-a-team-in-teamone)
