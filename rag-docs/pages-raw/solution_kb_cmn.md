###  Which ViewSonic ViewBoard model do I have?
Identify your device model through the following methods: 
  * Check the label on the back (typically on the right side) of the device and find the **model** name.
  * Go to your device's **Settings > About** and find the **model** name. (Contact your IT if you can't access your settings.)


  

##  Troubleshooting 
###  Add your device to myViewBoard Manager for easy configuration
Manage multiple installations of ViewBoards with 
  1. #### Sign in to myviewboard.com
With your myViewBoard Entity account, sign in and open the Manager console.
  2. #### Open Manager app on your device
Note the PIN displaying and input it into the Manager console to add the corresponding device.
  3. #### You're ready to manage devices!
Remotely power devices off, set a firmware update schedule, send broadcast messages, and more!


#### Unlock even more remote management features with Advanced licenses
Broadcast multimedia like YouTube, automate scheduled jobs, transfer files, and more, all to efficiently run your campus's devices!
