# Subscriptions
Use the **Subscriptions** page to review the entity's subscription details, including add-on services. 
  

  

  

  

## Access Subscription page
To access the **Subscription** page in your entity account, perform the following:
  

  1. Navigate to the following URL:
     * <https://cloud.viewsonic.com/entity>  
  

  2. Click **Sign in** on the upper right.
  3. On the next page, use your credentials to sign-in.
  4. The next screen should show your **Entity** page.
  5. Click **Subscriptions** on the side panel.


  

  

The **Subscription** page displays a list of all of the services your Entity account has been subscribed to. 
  

  * Click **Review details** to view more information about each item.
  * Click **Show more** to expand the details pane.
