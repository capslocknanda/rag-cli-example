# How to sign in to a ViewBoard using Companion
With certain models of ViewBoard, account sign-in may available. To sign in with your myViewBoard account, you can use the Companion app.
After first signing in to Companion, on the ViewBoard, do the following:
  1. On the ViewBoard's start-up screen, press **Sign In**.
  2. In your Companion app, open the QR code reader.
  3. Scan the QR code on the Sign In screen.
  4. Await for the sign-in to process.


ViewBoard sign-in screen, with QR code available on the right for users to sign in using their Companion app.
