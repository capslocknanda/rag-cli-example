# myViewBoard 3.0
Introducing myViewBoard! Say hello to a smarter, more intuitive whiteboarding experience. This new release is more than a visual refreshâ€”itâ€™s about giving you greater control, streamlining your workflow, and supporting every kind of learner in your classroom.
  

  

Key features
myViewBoard is a core component of ViewSonic's myViewBoard ecosystem, designed to enable interactive and collaborative experiences in both educational and professional environments. It provides tools for dynamic presentations, real-time collaboration, and digital content creation.
  

  

  * **Digital Whiteboarding  
** Offers a canvas for writing, drawing, and annotating using touch or stylus input. Supports multi-user interaction.  
  

  * **Cloud Integration  
** Connects with Google Drive, OneDrive, Dropbox, and other storage platforms for easy access to documents and files.  
  

  * **Multimedia Support  
** Allows insertion of images, videos, and web links directly into the whiteboard for enriched presentations.  
  

  * **Interactive Tools  
** Includes shape, text, and chart tools, along with an interactive pen for precision drawing.  
  

  * **Real-Time Collaboration  
** Users can connect remotely, interact with the whiteboard, and collaborate in real time from different devices.  
  

  * **Cross-Platform Compatibility  
** Available on Windows, Android, iOS, and browsers, making it accessible across multiple devices.
