## **Instant Snip and Share**
Capture Anything
Instantly snip any on-screen content including diagrams, websites, annotations, or key insights. ClassSwift lets you grab exactly what you need during fast-paced lessons, making it easy to highlight important material and keep students engaged without missing a beat.
Direct Delivery
Quickly share captured content with your entire class in seconds. ClassSwift sends materials directly to student devices, helping everyone stay aligned and focused. It’s a simple, efficient way to distribute key visuals and ideas while keeping your lesson moving smoothly.
## **Easy for Everyone**
Teacher-Friendly Simplicity
ClassSwift features an intuitive click-click-drag interface that’s easy to learn and use. Teachers can start creating and sharing content right away, without the need for extensive training or setup.
Seamless Access for Students
Students join classes quickly using QR codes or weblinks. This simple process works across all devices, making it easy for every student to participate without technical barriers.
Effortless IT Setup
ClassSwift integrates seamlessly with ClassLink for automatic rostering and easy deployment. Instantly sync student and teacher accounts without manual setup. It’s a hassle-free way to get your classroom up and running, saving time for IT teams. ClassSwift’s 1EDTech certification ensures that it’s built with the highest security standards to protect sensitive student information.
## **Spark Excitement**
Gamified Teaching
Encourage active participation with built-in point systems, buzzers, and countdown timers. ClassSwift makes lessons more engaging by turning learning into a game, helping students stay motivated and focused.
Multiple Ways to Engage
Capture attention and encourage participation with flexible tools like annotation, drawing, multiple choice questions, and audio responses. ClassSwift gives every student a voice and a way to engage, whether they’re visual, verbal, or hands-on learners—making class time more dynamic and inclusive.
## **Authentic Instruction and Genuine Student Response**
Comprehensive Teaching
The ClassSwift Hub provides access to detailed reports on assessments, attendance, and student participation. Teachers can monitor progress, identify learning gaps, and adjust instruction with confidence from one centralized platform.
Real-Time Student Insight
Access AI-powered analytics to track student participation, comprehension, and engagement. ClassSwift gives teachers instant visibility into how students are responding, making it easy to adjust instruction and support learning as it happens.
## **One Connected Ecosystem**
myViewBoard Software Integration
ClassSwift works natively with myViewBoard, delivering a smooth and intuitive experience. Teachers can snip and share content with just a few clicks, making it easy to capture and distribute materials on the fly while staying focused on instruction.

ViewBoard Interactive Displays

Teachers can interact directly with students and keep lessons clear, visual, and connected from one screen.
