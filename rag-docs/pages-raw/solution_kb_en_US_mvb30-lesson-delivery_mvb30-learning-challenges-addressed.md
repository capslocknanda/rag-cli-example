# Learning challenges addressed
myViewBoard 3.0 is designed to empower teachers to move beyond static, traditional classrooms by creating interactive and collaborative learning experiences. It supports a natural teaching flow, enabling educators to adapt in real time and deliver seamless lessons. The platform also integrates inclusivity features to support students with learning obstacles, ensuring that every learner can actively engage with the content.

## Subject Areas Covered
myViewBoard 3.0 is a versatile platform that can be applied across a wide range of subjects and teaching scenarios. Key features include:
  

  * **Math Tools** : Interactive resources for demonstration and explanation.
  * **Content Access** : Seamless integration with existing resources and websites.
  * **Assessment Tools** : Built-in features to create quizzes and conduct student evaluations.


  

These capabilities make myViewBoard 3.0 suitable for diverse subject areas and adaptable to different classroom needs.
