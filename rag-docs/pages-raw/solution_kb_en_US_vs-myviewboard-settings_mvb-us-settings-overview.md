# Settings overview
Use the myViewBoard settings page to customize your myViewBoard sessions. This page allows you to configure settings that will apply to your sessions every time you sign in to a myViewBoard session.
  

  

## Access myViewBoard settings
To access myViewBoard settings:
  1. Sign in to 
  2. On the landing page, locate the **myViewBoard** tile.   
Note the ellipsis icon when you hover over.
  3. Click the _more_vert_ icon and select **myViewBoard settings**.
  4. The **myViewBoard** settings section appears.


  

  

Alternatively, simply clicking on the **myViewBoard** tile opens the **myViewBoard Settings** page by default.
  

## Settings overview
The **myViewBoard** **Settings** page allows you to modify settings that are _automatically loaded_ when you use your account to sign-in to your **myViewBoard** sessions.
  

This section is organized into the following categories:
  

_discover_tune_ |  [**Preferences**](https://www.viewsonic.com/solution/kb/3184123)  
Customize session behavior when your myViewBoard account is loaded.   
---|---  
_bookmark_ |  [**Bookmarks**](https://www.viewsonic.com/solution/kb/3184125)  
Specify links that show up in the embedded browser's home page during your session.  
_imagesmode_ |  [**Backgrounds**](https://www.viewsonic.com/solution/kb/3184126)  
Specify a custom background image loaded after you sign in. Also allows you to specify additional images available for this feature during your session.  
_auto_fix_high_ |  [**Magic Line Pen**](https://www.viewsonic.com/solution/kb/3184127)  
Specify a custom image assigned to the Magic Line Pen after you sign in. Also allows you to specify additional images available for this feature during your session.  
_approval_ |  [**Shape Pen**](https://www.viewsonic.com/solution/kb/3184128)  
Specify a custom image assigned to the Shape Pen after you sign in. Also allows you to specify additional images available for this feature during your session.  
_cloud_ |  [**App & Cloud Integration**](https://www.viewsonic.com/solution/kb/3184129)  
Specify one or more cloud services that will be available during your session.  
  

After you sign out, myViewBoard reverts back to its default settings. Your custom settings are only loaded when you are signed in with your account.
