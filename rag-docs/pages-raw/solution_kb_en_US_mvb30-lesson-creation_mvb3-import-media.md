# Import media
Use myViewBoard to add online images and videos to grab your student's attention. myViewBoard makes this easy by having the following features built right in:
  

  

## Image Search
Opens a popup where you can search for images online to add directly into your canvas. Use to enhance your presentation with relevant visuals. 
  

  

  

  

## YouTube videos
Browse, play, and insert YouTube videos directly into your canvas. Allows you to create engaging lessons by integrating multimedia content. 
  

  

  

  

## Original content
Access a library of ready-made course materials, games, backgrounds, and lessons to download for immediate use. Most resources in Originals are designed for interactive learning.
