# Data alert
Use the **Data alert** section to configure alerts when certain conditions are met. Admins will receive an email when these conditions are met.  

## Setup alerts
The following alerts are available:
Alert name | Condition  
---|---  
Daily sign-in users | Notify when less than **n** users have signed in for more than **n** days.  
Power-on time by devices | Notify when **n** devices are turned on for more than **n** days.  
All alerts are turned off by default. To setup the alert:
  1. Access **Insights** via the ViewSonic dashboard.
  2. On the left panel, click **Data alert**.  

  3. Click on one of the 
    1. Specify parameters for this alert.
    2. Click **Save** to turn the alert on. 
    3. Click **Cancel** to go back and keep the alert off.   

  4. Back in the **Data alerts** section, the selected alert should now be active.
