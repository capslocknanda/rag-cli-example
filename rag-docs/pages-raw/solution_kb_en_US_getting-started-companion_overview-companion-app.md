# myViewBoard Companion overview
**myViewBoard Companion** is an app that allows teachers and students to interact with [myViewBoard Whiteboard](https://www.viewsonic.com/solution/kb/getting-started-whiteboard/overview-whiteboard), among other uses. It's available for iOS and Android devices. 
Use the myViewBoard Companion app to
  * Quickly and securely sign into [myViewBoard Whiteboard ](https://www.viewsonic.com/solution/kb/getting-started-whiteboard/overview-whiteboard)sessions by scanning a QR code.
  * [Enroll a Whiteboard instance into an entity](https://www.viewsonic.com/solution/kb/entity-management-1/software-instances#how-to-enroll-software-instances-1).
  * [Enroll a device into myViewBoard Manager](https://www.viewsonic.com/solution/kb/it-support-companion/add-devices-into-manager).
  * [Remotely control Whiteboard sessions](https://www.viewsonic.com/solution/kb/companion-and-whiteboard/sign-in-manage-whiteboard) with page navigation and a laser pointer.
  * Interact with other Whiteboard sessions by sharing files by [Throw ](https://www.viewsonic.com/solution/kb/student-engagement/throw).
  * Take part in polls and quizzes with [Pop Quiz](https://www.viewsonic.com/solution/kb/student-engagement/pop-quiz) [Polls ](https://www.viewsonic.com/solution/kb/student-engagement/polls).
