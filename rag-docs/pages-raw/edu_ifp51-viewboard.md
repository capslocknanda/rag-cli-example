Designed for Your School.
## ViewBoard® IFP51 Android™ EDLA-certified Interactive Displays
Powered by the latest Android™ EDLA-certified platform, the ViewBoard® IFP51 series interactive displays provide a secure and streamlined Google Classroom experience. Users can quickly sign in, access both Google and Microsoft files, and manage devices remotely. ViewSonic’s intuitive software integrates effortlessly with Google tools to enable dynamic lessons, foster student collaboration, and streamline classroom management.

## **High-Performance Multitasking**
**The octa-core CPU and 16GB RAM deliver smooth and efficient multitasking,** enabling teachers to run multiple applications simultaneously without lag.
## **Enhanced Multi-User Login**
In today’s dynamic classrooms, a single interactive panel can be used by multiple teachers throughout the day. ViewSonic’s EDLA-certified ViewBoard displays come with a custom launcher that simplifies this process through Enhanced Multi-User Login. Teachers can easily switch between guest mode and their personalized accounts, allowing them to access their files (such as Google Drive and Slides) across different classrooms. **This feature reduces the need for frequent IT support and enhances security with single sign-on and QR code login options.**
## **Immersive Audio for Maximum Engagement**
**A powerful 70W front-facing soundbar with a built-in subwoofer ensures crystal-clear sound throughout the classroom.** The integrated beamforming microphone array with noise cancellation captures voices from up to 8 meters away, making it ideal for remote teaching and lesson recordings.
## **Secure by Design with Android EDLA**
**Stay protected with built-in Google security, data encryption, and easy management tools** that help protect your network and devices.
[Learn More](https://www.viewsonic.com/edu/android-edla-security-solutions)
## **Native Access to the Google Education Ecosystem**
Integrate more useful resources and tools into your lesson plans by accessing hundreds of apps through the Google Play Store. With a single sign-on, teachers can **store and share files on Google Drive, edit files in Google Docs, share spreadsheets in Google Sheets, deliver presentations on Google slides and so much more**. Plus, teachers will enjoy access to the full Microsoft 365 suite (including Word, PowerPoint, Teams and more) from the Google Play Store.
## **Engaging Lessons with ViewSonic ® Education Software**
Support productivity with a suite of pre-installed ViewSonic solutions, including **myViewBoard ® for interactive whiteboarding, ClassSwift® for real-time classroom feedback, and ViewSonic AirSync™ for wireless content sharing.**
[Learn More](https://www.viewsonic.com/edu/software)
## **Device Management Made Better**
ViewSonic EDLA displays pair Google Workspace enterprise tools with ViewSonic Manager for complete control. **IT teams can manage apps, enforce policies, adjust hardware settings, and send campus-wide alerts, all from a single platform** that simplifies and streamlines device management across the school.
[Learn More](https://www.viewsonic.com/edu/viewsonic-manager)
