## **USB-C and Docking Monitors**
With RJ45 wired Ethernet connection, a USB hub for your accessories, and USB-C single- cable connectivity with up to 90W power delivery, **these all-in-one monitors eliminate the need for expensive docking stations** , and helps to reduce the TCO for your school.
[See USB-C Docking Monitors](https://www.viewsonic.com/edu/usbc-monitors)
## **Desktop Displays for STEM and STEAM**
Our monitors deliver **high resolutions, larger screen sizes and precise color accuracy** – making them ideal for STEM and STEAM labs. Whether it’s coding, design, or scientific analysis, ViewSonic’s commitment to quality ensures an enriching and productive learning experience.
[See our STEM and STEAM monitors](https://www.viewsonic.com/edu/professional-monitors)
## **Gaming Monitors**
Starting an esports program may seem daunting, but equipping your team with ViewSonic monitors takes a lot of the guesswork out. Featuring high resolutions, ultra-fast response times, flat or curved-screen designs, **ViewSonic ® gaming monitors are perfect for your school esports team or club**.
[Get Your Game On](https://www.viewsonic.com/edu/esportsforschools)
## **Digital Podium Displays**
Ideal for use in classrooms with existing projectors, the **ViewSonic ® myPodium™ ID2456 digital podium display allows teachers to annotate directly on their lessons, sketch ideas and share content with both in-class and remote students**. This gives teachers the freedom to interact with their students and improve discussions.
[Check Out myPodium Display](https://www.viewsonic.com/edu/id2456-old.html)
## **Portable Monitors**
Measuring about half-an-inch thick and weighing less than two pounds, our portable monitors can be stowed into backpacks and laptop cases – giving teachers and students **an additional screen to work effectively from nearly anywhere**.
[Explore Portable Monitors](https://www.viewsonic.com/edu/portable-monitors)
