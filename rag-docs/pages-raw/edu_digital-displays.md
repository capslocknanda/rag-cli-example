## **Versatile Display Solutions**
With **stunning 4K resolution, durable 16/7 and 24/7 operation ratings, and available in screen sizes ranging from 43” to 98”** , our CDE12 and CDE30 series of digital displays deliver the flexibility and features you need for classroom usage or general digital signage across campus.

## **Big-Screen, High-Impact Messaging**
With **huge 135”, 163” and 216” screen sizes, our Direct View LED displays make a sizable impression in any multipurpose room, student center or auditorium** , boasting seamless video wall images in Full HD and 4K resolutions. Our LDS135-151 is our foldable and portable 135” display that can be easily moved from room to room.
[Explore our Direct View LED displays](https://www.viewsonic.com/edu/led-displays)
## **Interactive Kiosks**
Perfect for up-close, high-impact messaging, ViewSonic all-in-one digital kiosks feature eye- catching images within sleek and slim designs. **Great for grabbing attention in busy, high-traffic areas** , these kiosks feature a scratch- resistant tempered glass faceplate for added durability.
[Explore our Interactive Kiosks](https://www.viewsonic.com/edu/kiosk-digital-displays)

ViewSonic Manager™ is a cloud-based management solution that empowers IT administrators to **remotely monitor devices, manage apps, push out broadcasts and more within the myViewBoard ecosystem**. Manager lets IT administrators seamlessly monitor and maintain individual displays across large organizations from a single control panel.
