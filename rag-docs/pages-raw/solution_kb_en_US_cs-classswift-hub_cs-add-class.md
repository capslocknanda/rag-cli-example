# Teacher View: Add Class
Before you begin an interactive session, set up your class and enroll your students in ClassSwift Hub.
**Instructions**
  1. On the **My Class** page, click **‚ÄúAdd Class.‚Äù**
  2. Decide how your students will join:


    * **Google Sign-In** ‚Äì students log in with their Google accounts.
    * **Guest Mode** ‚Äì students join without logging in.
    * Rostering Platform or Learning Management System ‚Äì we partnered with ClassLink and Google Classroom at the moment. These options will be available for paid customers.


**Option A ‚Äî Students Join with Google Sign-In**
Choose this option if you want students to sign in using their Google accounts.
  1. After clicking **‚ÄúAdd Class,‚Äù** enter your class details (Class Name and Number of Students).
  2. Under **‚ÄúRequire Students to Sign In,‚Äù** select **Google**. Make sure all students have an active Google email address.
  3. Click **Add** to create the class. 
  4. Open the class you just created and click **‚ÄúInvite Student.‚Äù**
  5. A pop-up will display a link to join ‚Äî share this with your students.
  6. When students click the link, they will log in with their Google account to participate.
