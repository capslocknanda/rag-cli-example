# Create a board
Boards in TeamOne allows users to brainstorm, diagram, and map ideas from scratch with easy-to-use building tools.
  

## After sign up
After completing the sign up process: 
  

1. You'll be prompted to select from several pre-defined board templates, or create an infinite blank board.
  

  

2. Click **Continue** to load your first TeamOne board. 
3. TeamOne will walk you through setting up navigation and grid style options, as well as provide a quick tutorial on basic TeamOne functions.
  

  

## Trackpad
## Gesture
## Grid
  

Select whether you want to use a mouse or a trackpad for navigation options.
  

TeamOne also supports finger gestures to perform various functions .
  

Select a grid style to help you align items on the board.
  

  

4. Once done, you're now ready to start collaborating on your TeamOne board!
  

  

## From the dashboard
On subsequent launches, TeamOne will lead you directly on the dashboard after you sign in. 
  

To create a new board from the dashboard: 
  * Click one of the templates to generate a new board based on that template.
  * Click **New board** and it should create a new blank infinite board.


  

  

  

  

## Update your board title
Click the board title to update the board name.
