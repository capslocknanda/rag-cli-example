# Overview
The **Overview** page is the default page that first appears when you access the ViewSonic Entity page. Use this page to review account information related to your entity.
  

  

  

## Access Overview page
To access the **Overview** page in your entity account, perform the following:
  

  1. Navigate to the following URL:
     * <https://cloud.viewsonic.com/entity>  
  

  2. Click **Sign in** on the upper right.
  3. On the next page, use your credentials to sign-in.
  4. The next screen should show your **Entity** page.
  5. The _account_balance_**Overview** section is shown by default.


  

  

## Entity information
Entity information available for review is given below. Note that all information presented on this page is read-only.
  

_account_balance_ |  **Entity name**  
Name of the entity registered during the entity creation process.  
---|---  
_id_card_ |  **ID**  
Unique ID assigned to this entity. You may be asked to provide this info when interacting with customer support.  
_domain_ |  **Main Domain**  
Main domain associated with this entity. To view, update or add additional domains, go to the Domain section.  
_person_outline_ _gpp_good_ |  **Owner**  
Email address of the user designated as owner during the entity creation process.  
_account_balance_ _density_medium_ |  **Entity list**  
Click **View** to see other entities this account can access.
