# Download and install Companion
## **System requirements**
To get Companion App on your device, make sure it meets the following technical requirements:
  * Android devices must be running **Android 5.0** (Lollipop) or higher.
  * Apple devices must be running **iOS 9** or higher.
  * Allow permissions for **camera** and **file access** if using the [Throw](https://www.viewsonic.com/solution/kb/student-engagement/throw) feature.
  * Android devices must not be in **Battery-Saver** /**Power-Saver** mode to allow Companion to function.


## **Downloading Companion**
  1. Go to either the **Google Play** or **Apple App Store** on your mobile device.
  2. Search **myViewBoard Companion**.
  3. Install the app and sign into your myViewBoard account (required to access all features).


### Direct download links
  

  1. On the device to which you want to download Companion, go to 
  2. Click **Download**.
  3. Locate the **Companion** download button.
  4. Click the download button for either **Google Play** or the **Apple Store**.
  5. When prompted, open your device's app store.
  6. Click the **install** button, and wait for the installation to complete.
