# Viewsonic Devices
The **ViewSonic Devices** section summarizes data related to devices in your entity. Note that the summary will only include data from devices that were successfully registered in your Manager console. By default, the information presented is based on activity from the last 90 days. 
This section is organized into several tabs. See the sections below for a quick description.
## Overview
The **Overview** tab presents statistics related to devices and licenses in your entity. 
Devices |  **Total devices**  
Devices in your entity that have been added to your Manager console.  
---|---  
**Daily active devices**  
Paired devices active in your entity per day. A device is considered active if it receives at least one action per day, either through the Manager console or on the device itself.  
Licenses |  **Total Advanced licenses**  
Total number of Advanced licenses in your entity, based on your subscription.  
**Devices with Advanced licenses**  
Percentage of devices assigned with an Advanced license.  
## Application
The **Application** tab presents statistics related to apps in your entity. 
  * **Top 10 apps across all devices**   
The most used apps while the ViewBoard input source was active.   

  * **App usage across devices**   
Number of devices that have used the selected app while the ViewBoard input source was active. 


Note that app usage is counted only on devices that meet the following criteria:
  * Registered with the entity's Manager console.
  * Connected to on an active network.
  * The **Enable to allow the ViewBoard to collect user behavior data** option is turned on. To activate this setting, access the Settings menu on the target device and navigate to:   

    * **Settings** Â» **Security**


## Utilization
The **Utilization** tab presents statistics related to how much power the devices are using in your entity.
  * **Total devices**   
Devices in your entity that have been added to your Manager console.  

  * **Power-on days**   
Counts the total consecutive number of days that saw a device in the entity being turned on.  

  * **Power-on time by devices**   
Displays a lists of active devices, and the days when these devices were turned on. Use the following icons to view more details:  
  



## Manager
The **Manager** tab presents statistics related to the use of the Manager app. By default, the information presented is based on activity from the last 90 days.
Command |  **Command types**  
Commands sent by category types in the Manager console.  
---|---  
Commands sent via the Manager console, organized by category type. Shows **All types** by default. Click a tile to change the details shown.
  * **Command types distribution**   
Displays a breakdown of commands, per type. Hover over an item to view a quick description. 
  * **Commands sent**   
Total commands sent for the selected period, broken down per day.

  
Broadcast | Provides a summary of all broadcast activity in your entity for the given period.  
**Total broadcasts by category** Breaks down the total broadcasts by types:  

  * **Total broadcasts**   
Total number of broadcast activities for the selected period.
  * **Message**   
Standard or urgent messages broadcasted to a ViewSonic device.
  * **Media**   
Any media broadcasted to a ViewSonic device.

  
**Distribution by broadcast type** Shows **All types** by default. Click a broadcast category to view the distribution of specific broadcast types to devices.
