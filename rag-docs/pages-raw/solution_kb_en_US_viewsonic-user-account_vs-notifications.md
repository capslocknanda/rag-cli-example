# Notifications
Update your **Notification** settings so you can receive regular new and updates about the ViewSonic ecosystem delivered straight to your inbox.
  

## Access Notifications settings
To access your **Notifications** settings directly, perform the following:
  

  1. Navigate to the following URL:
     * <https://cloud.viewsonic.com>  
  

  2. Click **Sign in** on the upper right.
  3. On the next page, use your credentials to sign-in.
  4. The next screen should show your **ViewSonic Account** page.
  5. Click **Notifications** on the side panel.


  

  

  

**via myViewBoard.com**
To access **Notifications** settings while logged in 
  

  1. Sign-in to myviewboard.com.
  2. Click your user avatar on the upper right.
  3. Click **Manage account** on the dropdown.
  4. Click **Notifications** on the side panel.


  

  

  

## Email Notifications
Specify if you want to receive the latest ViewSonic news via email. These will be sent to the contact email specified in the **Account** page. 
  

  

  

## Email verification required
Notifications will be sent only if the address provided as contact email has already been verified. Ensure that you have completed the verification process first before enabling notifications. 
  

To verify that the email address has completed the verification process: 
  

  1. Go to the **Account** section. 
  2. Review the **Contact email** field.
  3. Check for the 


  

Some accounts created via SSO may not have a contact email immediately available after creation. In such cases, perform the following before enabling notifications:
  

  1. Go to the **Account** section first. 
  2. Manually provide a valid email in the **Contact email** field.
  3. Complete the verification process.
