# AirSync Quick Start Guide
The **AirSync sender app** is used to share your screen, and can run on Windows, macOS, iOS, Android and the web.
  

If you need to download a PDF version, please check [this section here](https://www.viewsonic.com/solution/kb/en_US/send-your-screen/airsync-quick-start-guide#download-the-quick-start-guide-here).
  

  

## Quick Start
To start sharing: launch the AirSync app on the IFP, then follow the steps below:
  

  

| On your device, find and launch the AirSync sender application.  
---|---  
‚àü | Type the "Display Code" and "One-time password" shown on the IFP. The one-time password refreshes every 2 minutes.  
  

  

| Click "Next" then click "Share".  
---|---  
‚àü | For Windows, macOS, & web sender: Select the screen you want to share.  
For Android & iOS sender: The entire device screen will be shared.  
  

  

| That's it! Your screen shows up on the IFP instantly.  
---|---  
‚àü | To end screen sharing, click the ‚ÄúStop‚Äù icon on the AirSync sender application.
